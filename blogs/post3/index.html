Shared Study Room WeChat Mini Program

A Wechat Mini Program which allows students to book shared study rooms and find study partners.

Project from National Innovation and Entrepreneurship Training For College Students

Overview

Brief Introduction

Based on people’s thirst for knowledge in today’s society and the rise of the sharing economy, especially the increasing demand of college students for study rooms, we try to create a study room with a higher degree of freedom and more satisfying user experience. We refer to the existing shared study rooms on the market and some online learning apps, and try to combine them. At the same time, we designed and developed WeChat mini-programs and online study room models, and established an offline sand table model.

Through this project, we successfully applied for the National College Students’ Innovation and Entrepreneurship Training Course, and finally obtained the rating of the provincial project.

Highlights

  • The innovative smart study room uses a combination of online mini-program and offline physical sand table model to provide users with a good learning space
  • Personalized customization to meet customers with different needs on the basis of partitions
  • There is a mutual aid module for students, allowing users to make progress in the help of peers

This project was inspired by our group members’ learning experience at Beijing Jiaotong University. Since the school is located in Xizhimen, the second ring road of Beijing, it is a land where every inch of land is expensive. Therefore, after ensuring sufficient daily teaching resources, it is difficult for the school to provide more abundant study room resources. So we thought, what if a study room was designed from the perspective of students and users? Taking advantage of the opportunity of the National College Student Innovation and Entrepreneurship Project, we decided to implement the idea of a study room.

Considering the differences in the majors of the three members, in order to give full play to their respective professional advantages, a student from the School of Electrical Engineering and I was mainly responsible for developing small programs, and another student from the School of Architecture and Art was mainly responsible for the design of the construction model of the study room.

The shared study room is divided into three floors. The first-floor area has a public study room; the second-floor study room has a peer support room and a group discussion room; the third-floor area has a single-person study room and a customized room. The production of the mini program is also based on this. Users can enter the page and select their favorite study room to make an appointment. The reservation period generally starts from one hour, and the price of different study rooms is different.

02 Design Goals

  • Establish the electronic structure of the shared study room and design the room type
  • Determine the function of the mini program
  • Construct the sand table model of the physical shared study room

03 Design Results

WeChat Mini Program

I delivered the first version of UI in 7 days, constructing the basic functions. I would show the user-flow and demonstrate design details.

The page that is most suitable for user operation is adopted, combined with the UI design of general WeChat mini program, the function partition is set at the bottom of the page, and the user can complete all operations at the bottom of the mobile phone.

Users can add their favorite study room to their wishlist or pay directly. Before placing an order, you need to fill in the appointment time, appointment and phone number.
